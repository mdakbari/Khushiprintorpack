3 Ply Corrugated Boxes - Kani Packaging

3 Ply Corrugated Boxes have one fluted paper sandwiched between two line boards or 3 layers of papers. 3 Ply Boxes or Cardboard Boxes are made up of good quality boards and can easily handle 1 to 3 Kilograms of Weight.

5 Ply Corrugated Boxes - Kani Packaging

5 Ply Corrugated Boxes have two fluted paper sandwiched between three line boards or 5 layers of papers. 5 Ply Boxes or Cardboard Boxes are made up of good quality boards and can easily handle 5 to 8 Kilograms of Weight.

7 Ply Corrugated Boxes - Kani Packaging

3 Ply Corrugated Boxes have three fluted paper sandwiched between three line boards or 7 layers of papers. 7 Ply Boxes or Cardboard Boxes are made especially for heavy duty work and can easily handle 12 to 18 Kilograms of Weight.

9 Ply Corrugated Boxes - Kani Packaging

9 Ply Corrugated Boxes are nothing but a 9 layers of corrugated papers which gives strength to the boxes. We manufacturer best in quality 9 Ply Boxes which are used for heavy transit, shipment & exporting products at affordable prices.
