5 Ply Corrugated Boxes

5 Ply Corrugated Boxes consist of five layers of paper, providing enhanced strength and durability. These boxes are designed to handle heavier loads compared to 3 Ply Boxes. They can comfortably support weights ranging from 5 to 8 kilograms. At Khushi Print O Pack, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality 5 Ply Corrugated Boxes that are made from thick paper and heavy paper pulp, providing additional strength and protection for your products during transit.

Our 5 Ply Boxes are ideal for packaging heavier items, ensuring safety and durability throughout the supply chain. Whether you're shipping bulky items or requiring more robust protection, our 5 Ply Corrugated Boxes offer the best solution for your needs.

Advantages of 5 Ply Corrugated Boxes:

  • High Strength and Durability
  • Ideal for Heavy Product Packaging
  • Cost-Effective and Lightweight
  • Environmentally Friendly (Recyclable and Reusable)
