Printed Corrugated Boxes

Printed Corrugated Boxes, also known as Colored Corrugated Boxes, are made with multiple layers of paper and available in various colors, designs, sizes, and shapes. These custom-designed boxes are ideal for enhancing brand visibility and providing important product details in a graphical format, helping customers make informed decisions.

These printed packaging boxes are widely used by industries such as FMCG, Food and Beverage, Pharmaceuticals, Toys, and Home Appliances. With the ability to showcase logos and product information, these boxes make products more attractive and marketable.

At Khushi Print O Pack, we are one of the leading manufacturers of Printed Corrugated Boxes in Mumbai. Our factory in the Bhiwandi Industrial Area is equipped with state-of-the-art automatic and semi-automatic machines, ensuring the highest quality. Our skilled workforce enables us to deliver custom printed boxes that meet your exact needs.
