7 Ply Corrugated Boxes

7 Ply in Corrugated Boxes is nothing but the layers of Ply or Papers. 7 Ply Corrugated Boxes are made up of seven layers of papers and hence it's called 7 Ply Corrugated Boxes or 7 Ply Boxes. They are the most effective form of corrugated boxes which are highly durable and used for heavy transit and shipping.

At Khushi Print O Pack - We are one of the best 7 Ply Corrugated Boxes Manufacturers, Suppliers & Exporters in Ahmedabad, India. We offer 7 Ply Corrugated Boxes, Corrugated Sheets, and Corrugated Pads which are used for various packaging solutions.

Advantages of 7 Ply Corrugated Boxes / Cardboard Boxes:

  • High Durability
  • Low Cost
  • Low Weight
  • Recyclable and Reusable
